About Koi Dynasty

Taking care of all of your fresh and salt water fish needs

Koi Dynasty was established in 2012, but has been a dream of Keith Carter's long before then. Born and raised in Miami, FL, he has always had a love for salt water tanks and ponds at his own home. After years of successfully taking care of his own tanks and ponds and entrepreneurial background, he wanted to start a business of his own to help others who have a love for aquatics as well. Moving to the Antelope Valley, he realized that people wanted an oasis in the desert. Thus, Koi Dynasty was born!
